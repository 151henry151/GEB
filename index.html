Gödel, Escher, Bach: An Eternal Golden Braid

Douglas R. Hofstadter — An interactive companion

This site is a chapter-by-chapter companion to Hofstadter’s classic. Each chapter page offers simplified explanations at three reading levels and a unique interactive module that brings the chapter’s main idea to life. No quizzes—just tools and toys that let you experience the ideas.

Reading levels. On each chapter page you can switch between ELI5 (explain like I’m 5), ELI10 (like I’m 10), and ELI20 (like I’m 20). Pick the level that fits you; you can change it anytime.

Douglas Hofstadter has said he prefers readers use a physical copy of Gödel, Escher, Bach, since much of the book relies on complex typography and page design.
